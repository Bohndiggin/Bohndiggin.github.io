About Me:

Ever since I can remember, I've been obsessed with computers and tech. Video games have always been a part of my life. I remember playing Red Alert 2: Yuri's Revenge since I was like 11? I relaxed by taking apart old electronics. I spend my lunches watching dry reviews of new technology. (And I love it).


But besides tech, I'm a huge movie buff. Dune is my favorite book, and now, movie. Part one was excellent for many, many reasons. Someday I hope to get into the film industry, but for now, I'm happy tinkering with and using computers.


Speaking of tinkering, my new hobby is managing and making a digital collection of films. I decided to go with Plex because it's a bit easier than other options. But combining my love for hardware, software, and video has been very satisfying.


But something that's very important to me is interpersonal relationships. I'm very extroverted, and I love making people laugh.


A final quick thing of note is my love for Factorio. With over 800 hrs you could say I like it a bit. The most satisfying thing about Factorio is the simple, yet complex logistics management. It is always satisfying to get your gear production up by 2%. (Or more)


So yeah, that's me!
